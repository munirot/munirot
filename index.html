About Me

Hello There! here's a brief info of myself. I received a Computer Science degree from the University of Cambodia at the end of 2021. During my bachelor's year, I had six months of Web Development training from STEP Academy, as well as a two-week exchange program at Huawei's headquarters in China, which enhanced my technological and innovative understanding.

Meanwhile, I spent two and a half years during my third year of collage as a junior Front-end developer at IG Tech Group, an IT services and consulting firm. I spent the majority of my time there working with the Vue.js front-end web development framework. I also put in a lot of time working on backend development with Spring Boot and the Node.js framework.

In early 2022, I was accepted to enroll at Chungbuk National University in South Korea to pursue a Master's degree in Big Data. My research interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Object Detection, Data Analysis, and Data Mining.
