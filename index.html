About Me

Hello There! here's a brief info of myself. I received a Computer Science degree from the University of Cambodia at the end of 2021. During the second year of college, I received a scholarship for a had six-months long Web Development training from STEP Academy, as well as a two-week exchange program at Huawei's headquarters in China, which enhanced my technological and innovative understanding.

After completing the training from STEP Academy, I began working as a front-end developer at IG Tech Group, an IT services and consulting firm. I spent most of my time there working with the front-end web development framework Vue.js. After a year, I was able to transfer to the back-end team, where we mainly handled API development and database management with technologies like Node.js and Spring Boot.

In early 2022, I was accepted to enroll at Chungbuk National University in South Korea to pursue a Master's degree in Big Data. My research interests include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Object Detection, Data Analysis, and Data Mining.
